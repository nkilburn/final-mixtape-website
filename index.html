Episode 12 · The Beach Boys · 1966
God Only Knows
3:42
🎵
June 12, 2026· Episode 12· 42 min

Brian Wilson called it his finest work. Paul McCartney once said it's the greatest song ever written. "God Only Knows" is more than a love song—it's a baroque pop masterpiece built from layered harmonies, an unconventional structure, and an emotional depth that transcends decades.

In this episode, we dissect the Wrecking Crew's legendary recording session, the influence of Phil Spector's Wall of Sound, and Carl Wilson's unforgettable vocal take.

The Show

About Made You A Mixtape

One song, one episode, endless discovery. We take a single track each week and give it the attention it deserves—its history, the production, the stories that make it unforgettable.

Hosted by Alex Rivera, a former recording engineer turned storyteller, the show blends meticulous research with pure, infectious enthusiasm. Whether it's a 1950s doo-wop classic or a 1990s alt-rock anthem, every episode feels like discovering your favorite song all over again.

New episodes drop every Thursday. Grab your headphones—you're about to fall in love with music all over again.
